Pricing Questions Answered

Can I start with one module and add more later?
Absolutely. ResQ247 is fully modular. Start with patient management and billing, then add pharmacy, laboratory, telemedicine or any other module as your facility grows.
Is there a free trial available?
We offer a personalised 14-day trial for qualified facilities. Book a demo and our team will set up a sandbox with your facility's configuration.
Do county governments get special pricing?
Yes. Government and NGO organisations qualify for annual enterprise licensing with custom pricing based on facility count, population served and SLA requirements.
What currencies are supported?
Pricing is available in KES, USD, UGX, TZS and other regional currencies. We support local currency billing across East and West Africa.
